Join The Group

Become a member of the largest webbie group in the southeast.

AWDG is for anyone who makes Websites. Markup, style, code, typography, SEO, e-commerce, Web apps, and social networking are all valid topics here. We aim to provide a place for Web professionals to meet new people, exchange ideas, learn new things, and have a little fun. We have several events each month: one for learning, and happy hours all over the metro area for socializing.

The group features some of the best talent in the Web industry from all over the country. You are sure to leave each event with something that you can apply in your daily work. It is a great place to learn from your peers and colleagues, build new relationships, and grow within the local web community.
