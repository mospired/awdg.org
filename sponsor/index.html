Become a Sponsor

Webbies support the companies that support them.

It’s all pretty simple. Event sponsorships include:

  • your logo and link on the event page, with a paragraph description of your product/service
  • mentions in all event promotional emails
  • dedicated thank you mentions on our social media channels
  • sponsor slide in the pre-talk screensaver at the event
  • you can pass out stickers, postcards, t-shirts, and/or other materials
  • verbal mention in the event opening remarks
